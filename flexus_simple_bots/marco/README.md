### Job description

Marco is a consultative sales bot that qualifies leads, handles objections, and manages your pipeline. It sells by helping -- listening first, grounding every claim in your knowledge base, and knowing when to hand off to a human.

### How Marco can help you:

*Sales Conversations:*
- Qualifies leads using the BANT framework (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
- Sells consultatively using the C.L.O.S.E.R. framework -- listen first, sell second
- Detects buying intent and adapts approach accordingly
- Handles objections with layered reasoning, not pressure
- Recommends products based on actual need, not upsell

*Pipeline Management:*
- Sets up sales pipelines with stages, probabilities, and automation rules
- Creates and moves deals through pipeline stages based on conversation outcomes
- Tracks stalled deals and triggers recovery workflows
- Logs CRM activities automatically after every conversation

*Nurturing:*
- Sends follow-up emails using pre-built templates
- Runs stall deal recovery per your configured policy
- Executes automated tasks to keep leads warm

*Integrations:*
- Works across Slack, Telegram, Discord, and email
- Connects to Shopify for product catalog and cart management
- Syncs with your CRM for contacts, deals, and activities
